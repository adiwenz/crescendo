Privacy Policy for Crescendo

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Crescendo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Crescendo mobile application.

1. Overview

Crescendo is a vocal training application that provides guided singing exercises, real-time pitch feedback, and progress tracking. The app is designed to operate primarily on-device and does not sell or share personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Microphone / Audio Data

Crescendo requests access to your device's microphone (RECORD_AUDIO permission) solely to enable user-initiated vocal exercises, pitch detection, and optional audio recording features. Audio access is only active while an exercise is running.

We do not continuously listen, record, or access audio in the background.

Other Data

Crescendo may store locally generated exercise results, scores, and recordings on your device. These are created as a result of your direct interaction with the app.

3. How We Use Information

Audio input is used only to:

  • Detect pitch in real time during vocal exercises
  • Record practice sessions when you explicitly start a recording
  • Provide feedback and progress tracking features within the app

Audio data is not used for advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

4. Data Storage and Retention

By default, audio recordings and exercise data are stored locally on your device.
Crescendo does not automatically upload audio recordings to external servers.

If you choose to export, share, or back up recordings, that action is initiated explicitly by you.

You may delete recordings and exercise data at any time from within the app or by uninstalling the app.

5. Sharing and Third Parties

Crescendo does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
Audio data is not shared with third parties and is not used for ad targeting or analytics.

6. Permissions

Crescendo requests the following permission:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required only to perform vocal exercises and recordings initiated by the user.

You may revoke this permission at any time through your device settings. Some features may not function without microphone access.

7. Children's Privacy

Crescendo is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us so it can be removed.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data stored on your device. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Control microphone access through system permissions
  • Delete recordings and exercise data
  • Stop using the app and uninstall it at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact
